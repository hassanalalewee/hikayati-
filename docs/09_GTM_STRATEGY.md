# Hikayati — Go-To-Market Strategy

**Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** 2026-06-13

---

## 1. GTM OVERVIEW

**Launch Sequence:** Closed Beta → Soft Launch (GCC) → Full Launch (MENA) → Global Arabic

**Primary GTM Motion:** Product-Led Growth (PLG) with community amplification

**Core Message:**
> اجعل طفلك بطل قصته — قصص عربية تربوية شخصية تصنعها الذكاء الاصطناعي في دقائق

Translation: *Make your child the hero of their story — personalized Arabic educational stories created by AI in minutes.*

## 2. MARKET ANALYSIS

### Total Addressable Market (TAM)
- Arabic-speaking parents globally: ~50M households
- Digitally connected, smartphone-owning: ~25M
- Willingness to pay for children's digital content: ~5M (TAM = ~$750M/year at $150 ARPU)

### Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)
- Premium tier families in GCC + Levant + North Africa + Diaspora
- ~2.5M households (SAM = ~$375M/year)

### Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)
- 3-year target: 45,000 paying subscribers
- 3-year revenue target: ~$8.1M ARR (SOM = ~2% of SAM)

### Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Strength | Weakness |
|-----------|----------|---------|
| Generic AI story tools (English) | Existing user base | No Arabic, not personalized |
| Arabic children's apps (Lamsa, Qalam) | Arabic content | Not personalized, no story AI |
| Western parenting apps | UX quality | Not Arabic, not culturally relevant |
| Traditional Arabic publishers | Brand trust | No digital personalization |

**Hikayati's Moat:** Native Arabic + real child personalization + child development system = unique combination no competitor offers.

## 3. LAUNCH PHASES

### Phase 1: Closed Beta (Month 0–1)
**Goal:** Validate story quality with real families. Collect testimonials.

**Tactics:**
- Recruit 200 beta families through:
  - Arab mothers Facebook groups (>500K members each)
  - Instagram Arabic parenting accounts (DM partnerships)
  - Personal network + team families
- Free access in exchange for:
  - 3 story ratings per family
  - WhatsApp/video testimonial
  - Referral of 3 other parents
- Weekly feedback calls with 10 power users
- Track: story quality score, completion rate, delight moments

### Phase 2: Soft Launch — GCC (Month 1–3)
**Goal:** $7,500 MRR, 500 paying subscribers

**Target Regions:** Saudi Arabia, UAE, Kuwait (highest disposable income, tech adoption)

**Channels:**
1. **Arabic Instagram/TikTok** — organic content
   - "Story creation in real-time" videos (highest engagement)
   - Before/after: parent challenge → story solution
   - "Your child as a hero" transformation moments
   - Target: 10K followers, 5% conversion to trial

2. **WhatsApp Broadcast Lists**
   - Weekly story sample to 1,000-person list
   - "Share with one parent" CTAs
   - GCC has 95%+ WhatsApp penetration

3. **Influencer Partnerships (Micro)**
   - 20× Arabic parenting micro-influencers (50K–200K followers)
   - Each posts one personalized story for their child
   - Authentic reaction video format
   - Cost: free access + SAR 500–2,000 per post

4. **Arabic Facebook Groups**
   - "أمهات السعودية", "تربية الأبناء", "العائلة العربية"
   - Value-first content (share free article: "7 reasons stories change behavior")
   - Soft CTA to try free story

5. **Google Ads (Arabic)**
   - Target keywords: "قصص أطفال عربية", "تطبيق قصص أطفال", "تربية الأطفال ذكاء اصطناعي"
   - Budget: $2,000/month
   - Target CPA: $15

### Phase 3: Full MENA Launch (Month 3–6)
**Goal:** $75K MRR, 5,000 subscribers

**Additional Channels:**
1. **Levantine Markets** (Jordan, Lebanon, Palestine)
   - Levantine dialect available
   - Instagram + TikTok focus
   - Partnerships with Levantine parenting influencers

2. **Egypt**
   - Egyptian Arabic dialect launch
   - Egypt has the largest Arabic-speaking population
   - Lower price point (EGP pricing)
   - YouTube Arabic parenting channel sponsorships

3. **Arab Diaspora**
   - UK, USA, Germany, France
   - Target: Arabic parents worried about language and cultural identity loss
   - Message: "Keep your child's Arabic identity alive"
   - Facebook groups for Arab expats

4. **SEO Content Machine**
   - 100 Arabic blog articles: parenting challenges, development tips
   - 50 programmatic story landing pages (goal × age × style)
   - Target 500K monthly organic Arabic visitors by Month 12

5. **B2B Outreach (Schools)**
   - 50 international schools in UAE and Saudi Arabia
   - Kindergartens in GCC
   - Pilot program: 3 months free for teacher feedback

## 5. CONTENT MARKETING STRATEGY

### Arabic Blog Themes (target keywords)
1. "كيف تعلم طفلك الصدق" (How to teach your child honesty)
2. "قصص أطفال عربية للنوم" (Arabic bedtime stories for children)
3. "تطبيقات تعليمية للأطفال العرب" (Educational apps for Arab children)
4. "كيف تبني ثقة طفلك بنفسه" (How to build your child's self-confidence)

6. "تحديات التربية في زمن الشاشات" (Parenting challenges in the screen age)

**Content format:** 1,200–2,000 word articles in MSA, optimized for Google Arabic

**Social content:** Short-form video (Instagram Reels, TikTok) showing actual story creation process

```
Day 0:  Welcome — "قصتك الأولى تنتظرك" (Your first story awaits)
Day 1:  "هكذا تصنع حكايتي القصة المثالية" (How Hikayati creates the perfect story)
Day 3:  "10 مشكلات تربوية تحلها القصص" (10 parenting problems stories solve)
Day 7:  Upgrade CTA — "جرب بلا حدود" (Try unlimited)
Day 14: "أطفال عائلة [X] تغيروا بفضل القصص" (Social proof)
Day 30: Winback — Free story coupon
```
